PCEA BARAKA

PCEA Baraka Church is a Presbyterian church located in Mugutha, Ruiru.The English service begins at 8:00 AM, offering a time of praise, prayer, and reflection for the English-speaking congregation. Following this, a Kikuyu service is held at 10:00 AM, catering to those who prefer worship in the Kikuyu language.
